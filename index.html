Javanese Culture

A Blend of Traditions

Java is home to diverse ethnic groups with unique traditions, arts, and languages. The island is known for its:

  • Wayang shadow puppetry
  • Gamelan music ensembles
  • Batik textile art
  • Traditional Javanese dance
